Rise To Power

A political influence text based adventure game

Welcome to Rise To Power! Your name is President Chuckle McLaughster of the intergalactic nation of Nova Terra. Make decisions in favor of the people or in favor of the current government. The choice is yours! Choose the text box that best represents what you would do in each situation. But Be careful! Your choices will affect your reputation with the people and/or your reputation with the government. If your reputation with the people gets too low, you will be impeached. If your reputation with the government gets too low, you will be assassinated. Get either score to 100 and you win! Good luck!
